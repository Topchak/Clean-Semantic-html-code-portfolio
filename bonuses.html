Bonuses & Promotions

What is a Wagering Requirement?

A Wagering Requirement tells you the value of bets you need to place during a specific time and may apply to various situations. A deposit needs to be wagered before a withdrawal can be requested, a bonus needs to be wagered to release any winnings from the bonus balance to the cash balance, and so on.

Please note that the wagering contribution may differ depending on the game. Details can be found in the general Terms & Conditions.

Example:

  • You receive an offer for a 50% deposit bonus with a wagering requirement of x35, a validity period of 10 days, and make a 50 EUR deposit.
  • To calculate the wagering requirements, add the deposit and bonus amount and multiply by 35, so: (deposit + bonus) x 35.
  • In this case (50 EUR + 25 EUR) x 35 = 2,625 EUR
  • To complete the bonus wagering requirements, you will need to place bets in the value of 2,625 EUR within ten days.

Where can I find information on available bonus offers and promotions?

How do I claim and activate a bonus?

To claim a bonus, simply follow the instructions of the promotion to qualify and receive the offer. Please visit the promotions page for more information.

After you make a qualifying deposit, a notification should pop up to let you know that a bonus has been claimed, and you will need to click on the ‘Activate’ button.

If the notification does not appear, or if you close it, you should still be able to find your bonus in your profile section and activate it from there.

In case the bonus does not automatically become available for activation in your profile after your qualifying deposit, contact Customer Support before playing with the deposit to request a manual credit.

How can I use Free Spins?

Log in to your account, and after you activate or manually get Free Spins credited to you, open the slot on which your spins are available. Most games will notify you about the number of Free Spins available before you start playing.

After you play through your batch of Free spins, any winnings will be added to your bonus balance and will need to be wagered according to the promotion’s requirements.

Example: If the wagering requirement is x40, and you have won 10 EUR, you will need to wager 400 EUR (10 EUR winning x 40 wagering requirement).

For more information, check out the Terms & Conditions of the relevant promotion.
