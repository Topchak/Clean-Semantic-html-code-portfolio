My Account & Verification

How long does it take to get verified?

Reviewing verification documents can take 1-3 business days from the time that the requested documents have been received. Please note that delays in the processing timeframe may occur in case incorrect documents have been submitted or further checks need to be completed.

How can I upload documents?

To upload your documents for verification, log in to your account and go to your profile. There, you should find the 'Verification' section, from where you can follow the steps to upload your documents.

Alternatively, you may attach the documents to an email and send them to our KYC Department.

How can I reopen my account?

If you wish to reopen your account, please contact Customer Support via Live Chat or email for assistance.

Which documents are needed for the verification process?

The standard set of documents needed for an account verification are:

  • Photos of your Identity (ID card, driving license, or passport) with all four corners visible. Screenshots will not be accepted.
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, government letter, TV/internet bill in your name and not older than three months)

Please note that additional documents may be requested to complete the verification procedure.

Can I have more than one registered account?

Players can have only one account on our site. Every other account that the same person creates will be recognized as a duplicate one and closed at some point (you can read more about the policy of duplicate accounts in our general T&C).

How can I deactivate my account?

To deactivate your account, you can contact our Support team via Live Chat or email, and they will assist you.

Please also check the Responsible Gaming section in your profile, where you will read more information.

Where can I edit my personal information?

If you want to update personal details on your account, kindly contact our Finance department at kyc@Fat Pirate.com. They are going to guide you accordingly.

I'm unable to log in to my account. What is the issue?

Double-check that the username/email address and password you entered are correct. The password is case-sensitive, so make sure your Caps Lock key is turned off.

If you have recently changed your password and can't access your account, please clear the Cookies & Cache within the internet browser or try using a different browser.

If you are still experiencing difficulties logging in to your account, don't hesitate to contact our Customer Support Team for further assistance. To assist you faster and more efficiently, please ensure you take a screenshot of the error message and attach it to your request.

Why am I not able to open an account?

You might not be able to open an account if:

a) your country belongs to the restricted jurisdiction list (you can find more information about the list in the general T&Cs of the website)

b) if you already created an account previously

c) you are less than 18 years old

Please contact our Customer Support Team if you don’t recall registering an account but have any doubt regarding this. You can reach us via Live Chat and E-mail support@Fat Pirate.com.

How can I join website?

To join our website, you would need to make sure that this is your first-ever account with us, and you would need to follow the registration procedure.

I forgot my user name/registered email address. What should I do?

You need to contact us via Live chat/e-mail, and one of our customer support agents will assist you with sending a reminder.

I forgot my password, how can I recover it?

Please click on the forgot password button, write your registered email address in the pop-up window, and you will receive an email for resetting your password.

If you don't receive an email, please contact our support team, who will send a new password to your registered email.
