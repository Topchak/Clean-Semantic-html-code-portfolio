What is 'Return to Player'?

Return to player (otherwise known as 'RTP') indicates the percentage of any wagered money paid back to players over time from slots. Calculated as an average established on millions of plays, it's also an indicator of the long-term performance a player could expect to see from playing that particular game - but of course, this doesn't account for what could happen in the short term.
