Responsible gaming

Our commitment to responsible gambling is directly related to the customer service policy. The company wants you to get pleasure from the games that we provide, but at the same time be aware of the possible social and financial harms associated with gambling problem.

Maintaining control

Gambling should be seen as a fun and exciting way to spend time, and not as an income. It is difficult for some players to perceive gambling only as a means of relaxation and risking only what they can afford, as most players do.

To maintain control and prevent gambling addiction, you always need to consider the following:

  • You should take gambling not as a way to get income, but as way to spend leisure time;
  • You should not seek immediate refund, as you always have a chance to win another time;
  • Play only if you have "extra" funds;
  • Pay attention to the time and the amount of money spent in the game;
  • If you think that you need a break from gambling, you can contact the support team and we will exclude you for a while;
  • Self-exclusion request: you can contact Support Service Team via e-mail: support@Fat Pirate.com, and we will close your account within next 24 hours. Responsibility

If you are worried that gambling has a negative impact on your life or lives of your close ones, the following questions can help you figure it out.

  • Does gambling distract you from going to work or school?
  • Is gambling to you simply a way to spend some time or avoid boredom?
  • Do you play alone for long periods?
  • Have others ever criticized your gambling?
  • Have you lost interest for family, friends and hobbies because of gambling?
  • Have you ever lied to cover up the amount of money or time spent on gambling?
  • Have you ever lied, stole or borrowed money for purpose to get money to gamble or to pay gambling debts?
  • Are you reluctant to spend ‘gambling money’ on anything else?
  • Do you play until you lose all your money?
  • Do you try to recoup as soon as possible right after loss?
  • Do you feel lost and desperate when you run out of money while gambling?
  • Can any arguments, frustrations or disappointments make you want to play?
  • Do you feel depressed or even suicidal because of your gambling?

If your answer to most of the questions is yes, there is a possibility that you have gambling problems. In order to discuss this issue, you should contact one of the organizations listed below, which will be able to give you advice and support.
